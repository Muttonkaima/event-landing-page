FAQ

Everything teams ask
before they launch.

A quick look at how Conclave supports organizations from onboarding and event setup to event-day operations and post-event follow-up.

How does an organization get started on Conclave?

We provide platform access to organizations, create credentials for the authorized team, and send those login details securely through email so they can start inside the platform right away.

What can teams configure when creating an event?

Teams can create an event with branding, color themes, font styles, sessions, registration duration, attendee limits, ticket types including paid, free, and complementary options, and all the core setup needed before publishing.

Can we manage registration forms, emails, badges, and ticket delivery?

Yes. Teams can build registration forms, customize email templates like confirmation, reminder, invitation, and thank-you emails, design badge templates, and send attendees QR-coded tickets after secure payment and confirmation.

How do event managers, permissions, and attendee operations work?

You can add event managers, assign permissions per event, and let each manager log in with access only to the capabilities they are meant to manage. Teams can also handle attendees, ticket scanning, check-ins, benefits assignment, and redemption flows.

What screen and speaker tools are included?

Conclave supports screen creation, slideshow and image management, per-screen controllers, and a multi-screen view showing status and active content. Speakers can log in to their dashboard, upload session resources, and have those assets shown during their sessions.

Can we run campaigns and collect feedback after the event?

Yes. Teams can create blast, scheduled, trigger-based, event-based, and manual email campaigns, publish and end events, enable post-event feedback forms, review responses, and monitor analytics for revenue, ticket purchases, check-ins, and attendee activity.
